About Us

Syntax (Group -3)
We are a dedicated team of students from the MIT LAP Program, currently in our third year as Computer Science majors. Together, we are developing the Hymn Myanmar project, a passion-driven initiative to bring timeless hymns to the digital age. Our mission is to create a seamless and accessible platform for everyone to explore, enjoy, and be inspired by these sacred melodies.
